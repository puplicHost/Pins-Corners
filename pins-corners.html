Pins & Corners

About Us

Pins & Corners is a forward-thinking real estate developer born from a legacy of three decades in international business. Founded by brothers Hamdy and Amr Abd ElLatif, the company channels a heritage of excellence in import, export, and manufacturing into the creation of landmark destinations. We specialize in identifying and transforming strategic geographic points—Pins—into vibrant, lasting community hubs—Corners. Our philosophy, "Purposeful Pins, Lasting Corners," guides every decision, from location selection to final design, ensuring each project delivers timeless value and fosters genuine human connection. With a solid financial foundation and the support of key international partnerships, we focus on developing modern, mixed-use projects that blend premium design, commerce, leisure, and community living. Our work begins in Egypt with our flagship project, WESTWIND, in Sheikh Zayed, with a vision to expand regionally across the Middle East. We are more than builders; we are place-makers committed to elevating the real estate landscape through visionary design, sustainable practices, and an unwavering dedication to integrity and excellence.

Hamdy Abd ElLatif & Amr Abd ElLatif

Founders, Pins & Corners

Our story began in 1994, rooted in the values of quality, partnership, and strategic growth cultivated over three decades in the Food & Beverage industry. That journey across continents taught us that true success is built on trust, perseverance, and a vision that looks beyond the immediate horizon. Today, with Pins & Corners, we are translating that legacy into a new language—the language of space, community, and lasting impact. We believe real estate is not just about constructing buildings, but about creating destinations that inspire, connect, and endure. Each project is a carefully chosen pin on the map, destined to become a cornerstone of community life and economic vitality. Our leadership is driven by a commitment to innovation, integrity, and sustainable growth. We are dedicated to developing state-of-the-art projects that marry exceptional design with long-term value, always mindful of our responsibility to our investors, partners, and the communities we serve. The launch of WESTWIND in Sheikh Zayed marks the first realization of this vision. It is the blueprint for our future—a future where we continue to pin the corners that will define tomorrow. We invite you to join us on this journey of discovery and creation.

Our Vision

To pin the corners that shape tomorrow as a leading real estate developer across Egypt and the region, creating landmark destinations where design meets purpose. We aim to leave our lasting mark by creating timeless mixed-use projects built on intentionality, innovation and design excellence.

Our Mission

To transform strategic pins on the map into lasting corners where communities and businesses prosper in Egypt and beyond. Through visionary design, purposeful planning, and sustainable development, we are committed to deliver destinations that elevate communities, inspire growth, and stand the test of time.
