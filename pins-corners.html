About Us

CRAFTING LEGACY THROUGH ARCHITECTURAL PRECISION

A Heritage of Excellence

Pins & Corners is a forward-thinking real estate developer born from a legacy of three decades in international business. Founded by brothers Hamdy and Amr Abd ElLatif, the company channels a heritage of excellence in import, export, and manufacturing into the creation of landmark destinations.

Founded on the principles of integrity and innovation, our firm has consistently pushed the boundaries of traditional real estate, introducing concepts that harmonize luxury living with architectural elegance.

Our story began in 1994, rooted in the values of quality, partnership, and strategic growth cultivated over three decades in the Food & Beverage industry. That journey across continents taught us that true success is built on trust, perseverance, and a vision that looks beyond the immediate horizon.

Our Vision

To pin the corners that shape tomorrow as a leading real estate developer across Egypt and the region, creating landmark destinations where design meets purpose.

Our Mission

To transform strategic pins on the map into lasting corners where communities and businesses prosper in Egypt and beyond through visionary design and purposeful planning.

Notable Achievements

Awarded "Luxury Developer of the Year" 2024 for innovative desert-modern aesthetics.

Successfully delivered over 10+ premium residential complexes in New Cairo and North Coast.

Pioneers in integrating smart with "Green Giant" technology across all masonry projects.
