OUR PROJECT — WESTWIND

WESTWIND – Pinning the First Corner in the Heart of Zayed

Our debut flagship project is located on El Bostan Road in Sheikh Zayed. This prime corner offers unmatched accessibility and visibility.

Type Mixed-Use Destination
Size 40,000 sqm

Features: A curated selection of sought-after retail brands, distinctive restaurants, and dynamic office spaces.

Design Inspiration: Carved by nature, inspired by the fluidity of desert winds and sand dunes.

Concept: An urban oasis featuring landscaped palm groves and water features, seamlessly connecting retail, dining, and workspace experiences.
