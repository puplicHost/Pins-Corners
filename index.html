Architecture

Architecture

Our design philosophy is inspired by nature. Like ventifacts—stones sculpted by desert winds—our architecture captures the fluid essence of Egypt's landscapes. Buildings emerge as textured masses reminiscent of sand dunes, creating a sense of movement and harmony with the environment.

Interior & Urban Design

Interior & Urban Design

We design from the inside out and the outside in. Our interiors extend the external narrative, using natural materials and fluid layouts. Our urban design prioritizes walkability, community integration, and creating vibrant, connected neighborhoods.

Landscape

Landscape

We craft urban oases. Inspired by desert palm groves, our landscapes feature intricate planting and serene water features that tie the environment together, offering a calming retreat in the heart of the city.
